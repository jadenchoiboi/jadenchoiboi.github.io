A random recipe: how to make lemonade

Collect these Ingredients

You will have to collect all these ingredients to properly make this. :D

  • pure filtered water
  • lemon juice
  • sugar
  • stevia leaf
  • extract
  • Natural Flavours
  • Lemons

    • After you have collected all these ingredients, follow these steps correctly

  1. Squeeze the lemons to make one cup of lemon juice. Try not to squirt lemon juice into your eyes since it WILL hurt.
  2. After that, find a gallon pitcher
  3. If that is done, combine the cup of lemon juice, 1 cup of sugar, and 6 cups of cold water
  4. Put ice in it and your done

    5. WHAT THIS DRINK IS

    1. This drink is lemonade, which is commonly made by little children and sold on stands on Summer days for money.
